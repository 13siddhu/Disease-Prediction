About the Project

This is an innovative project that combines machine learning with healthcare to help people identify potential diseases based on their symptoms. Our goal is to make healthcare more accessible and provide valuable information to users about their health conditions.

Our Mission

To create a user-friendly platform that helps people understand their symptoms and get appropriate medical attention when needed. We believe in leveraging technology to improve healthcare accessibility and awareness.
